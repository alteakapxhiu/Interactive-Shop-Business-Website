The Chef Dimitris Skarmoutsos

Pane e Souvlaki opened its doors in February 2012. Since then, with its unique flavours, it has managed to become a favourite choice for those who have high expectations from their Souvlaki!! We owe these unique flavours to the famous chef Dimitri Skarkoutso who created the menu and helped us create your go to Pane e Souvlaki. We offer a selection, of juicy grilled meats, salads and appetizers to satisfy all ages and provide a high quality meal, respecting every dietary need (vegetarian, vegan). At Pane e Souvlaki quality is our priority, all our ingredients are fresh and handpicked, our Souvlaki, burgers and everything is prepared daily in our family-run restaurant. We are open all year round and we our looking forward to having you!
